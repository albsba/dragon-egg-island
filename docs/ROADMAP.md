# Dragon Egg Island -- Roadmap

## Stage 1: Egg Collecting (Current)
- Two-player co-op movement (Harper + Audrey)
- Flower Meadow and Heart Forest zones
- Fire, Nature, and Love dragon eggs
- Hatching animations, baby dragon parade
- Adventure mode (unlock zones) + Free play mode
- Collection tracker UI

## Stage 2: More Zones & Dragons
- Enchanted Forest (Nature, Shadow eggs)
- Crystal Cave (Star, Ice eggs)
- Snowy Mountain (Ice, Star eggs)
- Volcano Ridge (Fire, Shadow eggs)
- Rainbow Falls -- secret final zone (Rainbow eggs)
- 7 total dragon types

## Stage 3: Dragon Riding & Flying
- Mount hatched dragons to fly around the map
- Faster movement, access elevated areas
- Pick which dragon to ride from your collection

## Stage 4: Dragon Powers
- Fire dragon: melts ice blocks
- Nature dragon: grows bridges over gaps
- Love dragon: heals/helps
- Shadow dragon: reveals hidden paths
- Star dragon: lights dark areas
- Ice dragon: freezes water to walk on
- Rainbow dragon: special/all powers
- Puzzle elements in zones that require specific powers

## Stage 5: Polish & Extras
- Sound effects and music
- Save/load progress
- More egg types (the girls want LOTS)
- Possible migration to Phaser.js for richer gameplay
